Our Story

Bringing Hidden Gems from Japan to You

Japan Finds started with a simple passion: uncovering the unique, high-quality items tucked away in Japan’s local markets, traditional workshops, and bustling city streets. We realized how difficult it was for people outside of Japan to access these authentic pieces without outrageous shipping fees or confusing processes.

That’s why we do the hunting for you. From limited-edition collectibles to everyday lifestyle pieces, we handle the sourcing, quality checks, and importing—bringing a piece of Japan straight to your home.
